Release notes

 

jGallery - PHP based photo album viewer 1.3

 

You can view a live demo of jGallery at; http://portal.kooijman-design.nl/jGallery/

 

 

 

Download, installation & support forums:

You can visit the forums at; http://portal.kooijman-design.nl/viewforum.php?f=1

 

 

Installation:

Upload all files in the *.zip-package to your server, after this you can simply upload images and folders to the ‘albums/’-directory.

 

In the config.inc.php file you can change some settings for your gallery.

 

You can download themes at http://portal.kooijman-design.nl/viewtopic.php?t=36

Simply upload the theme-folder to jGallery/themes/ and change the theme setting in config.inc.php.

 

jGallery requires PHP5 and GD-library to be installed on your server.

 

 

Features:

jGallery provides you with a menu with which you can brows your photo’s online. The menu shows you your current position and you can always switch back home.

 

You can change themes, cols and rows in your gallery and thumbnail sizes.

 

jGallery can be included from a higher directory and will function the same.

 

You can install a existing theme or edit the one you have installed by editing the stylesheet and theme-configuration file.

 

 

Usage:

To include jGallery in another page simply use this line:

<?php include('jGallery/index.php'); ?>

 

By editing style and configuration you can tune jGallery up to fit into your website’s template exactly.

 

 

Enjoy!
